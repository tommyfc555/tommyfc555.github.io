Discord OAuth Setup

Follow these steps to configure OAuth:

Step 1: Get Your Client ID

Go to Discord Developer Portal → Your App → OAuth2 → Copy "Client ID"

Step 2: Add Redirect URL

In "OAuth2" → "Redirects", add:
https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io
https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/
